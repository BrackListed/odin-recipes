How to make steak?

  1. Let the steak rest for 30-60 minutes then pat it dry with a paper towel
  2. Cook the steak in room teperature
  3. Season the steak generously
  4. Heat the pan
  5. Sear the steak for 2-3 minutes without moving
  6. Lower the heat, addd butter, smashed garlic and othr herbs. BAste the melting, fragrant butter over the steak for another minute
  7. Transfer the steak into a cutting board and let it rest for 5-10 minutes
  8. Slice the steak and enjoy
