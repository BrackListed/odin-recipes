How do you make chicken?

Firstly, what you need:

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Cold Water
  • Cornstarch
  • Oil
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Ground Powder
  • Garlic Pepper

How to make chicken:

  1. Clean the chicken pieces with water and pat them dry
  2. Season the chicken
  3. Let the chicken rest for 20 minutes
  4. Prepare a bowl with flour and a pinch of salt and pepper
  5. Coat each piece of chicken with the flour mixture
  6. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat until hot but not smoking
  7. Carefully place the chicken into the oil
  8. Fry each side until golden brown and fully cooked inside
  9. Remove the chicken and place it on a paper towel to drain excess oil
  10. Let it rest for a few minutes and then enjoy
